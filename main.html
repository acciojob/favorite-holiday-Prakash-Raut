Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto blends timeless tradition with modern comfort—think ancient temples, serene gardens, and tea houses tucked into quiet lanes. It’s a city where culture and nature meet in every season, from cherry blossoms in spring to fiery maples in autumn.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Taisha — walk through thousands of vermillion torii gates.
  2. Kiyomizu-dera — a hilltop temple with a panoramic wooden stage.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove — an otherworldly pathway of swaying stalks.
  4. Gion District — historic streets where traditional teahouses still thrive.
  5. Philosopher’s Path — a tranquil canal walk lined with cherry trees.

Must-Try Foods

  • Kaiseki — a seasonal, multi-course meal with artful presentation.
  • Yudofu — silky tofu hot pot served in Zen temples.
  • Matcha Sweets — wagashi and pastries infused with ceremonial green tea.
  • Obanzai — Kyoto home-style dishes emphasizing local produce.
  • Soba/Udon — handmade noodles with umami-rich broth.
